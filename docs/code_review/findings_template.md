# Findings Template

**Format:**
`ID | Severity | Category | Location (file:line) | Evidence | Impact | Suggested direction | Confidence | Cross-ref`

*   **ID:** e.g., F-1.2-01 (Phase 1.2, Finding 01)
*   **Severity:** Critical | High | Medium | Low
    *   *Weighting precedence: Ownership/Data Integrity > Privacy/Keys > Cost > Reliability/UX*
*   **Category:** Reliability | Security | Privacy | Data-integrity | Correctness | Performance | Maintainability | UX | Compliance | Observability | Test-gap
*   **Location:** e.g., `backend/src/auth.ts:45`
*   **Evidence:** How/why it's broken.
*   **Impact:** What the adversary gains, or what fails.
*   **Suggested direction:** A pointer to the solution, NOT a fix.
*   **Confidence:** High/Medium/Low
*   **Cross-ref:** Links to other related findings.
